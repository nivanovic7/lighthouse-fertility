Navigating the way to success.

Lets take the first steps of commercialising your company together.

Your partner for growth in the IVF industry

Hi, I’m Simon! With over 15 years in the women's health and MedTech industry, I’ve worked with top manufacturers and high-profile startups in the IVF space. Now, through Lighthouse Fertility, I bring that experience to help MedTech startups navigate the complexities of the IVF market.

How I Help You

  • Market Research & Customer Discovery
    I use direct engagement with your target audience—whether B2B or B2C—to gather invaluable feedback that shapes product development, pricing strategies, and market messaging. Through qualitative research and surveys, I help you understand your market, uncover unmet needs, and refine your approach.
  • Go-to-Market Strategy & Execution
    I work closely with your team to create a custom go-to-market plan that aligns with your product’s unique value proposition. Whether you’re targeting healthcare professionals or IVF patients, I help you identify the best channels and strategies for launch, ensuring maximum impact.
  • Business Model Development & Strategy
    I help you build a solid business model that outlines your product’s key components, target markets, revenue streams, and marketing strategies, ensuring your business is set up for sustained growth and success.
  • Sales & Revenue Growth
    I turn early research collaborations and customer insights into actionable sales strategies that drive early revenue. By targeting the right customers and partners, I help you build momentum and scale faster.
  • New Market Access
    I help you expand in to new markets, either through direct sales or channel partners carefully selected to represent your business. In cases where regulatory approval is required, I will set you up with right advice to enter these markets.
  • Team building & transition
    At the right time I will transition my responsibilities to a commercial team within your organisation. My track record of successful team building and appointment stands in good stead to help you build a team that will take you to the next phase of commercial expansion.

Inspired
